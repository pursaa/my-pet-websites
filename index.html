Sugar Gliders

Where do sugar gliders come from

Sugar gliders are little marsupials. They were originally found in Australia and Indonesia and have been domestically bred as household pets in the United States for the last 12-15 years. They are in the same general family as a Kangaroo or Koala Bear.

What do sugar gliders like

  • They like to run free and glide
  • They love sweets
  • They like puzzles
    • open containers
    • dig in a box
    • play hide and seek
  • They like night time
  • They like to play with other animals
